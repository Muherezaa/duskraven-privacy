Duskraven Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 02, 2025

At Duskraven, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Info: Email, phone (optional), profile name
  • Content: Posts, messages, product listings, photos you upload
  • Usage: App opens, feature usage (no personal tracking)
  • Location: Only if you enable for job search (optional)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Run the app (show listings, enable chat, process bookings)
  • Improve features (anonymous analytics)
  • Moderate content (remove spam/abuse)
  • Send notifications (if enabled)

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No sale of personal data
  • No ads or third-party analytics

4. Sharing & Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud, Uganda region). We only share with:

  • Service providers (hosting, moderation)
  • Legal requirements (if required by law)

5. Your Rights

  • Access or delete your data
  • Stop notifications
  • Delete account in-app (Settings → Log Out → Delete)

Account and Data Deletion

Users have the right to delete their accounts and all associated data at any time. When you request account deletion through the app (via Settings → Log Out → Delete Account), we will permanently remove your personal information, including account details, posts, messages, and any uploaded content from our servers. This process is irreversible and complies with applicable data protection laws. Note that some anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics purposes to improve the app, but it will no longer be linked to you. If you have concerns or need assistance with deletion, contact us directly.
